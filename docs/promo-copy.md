## 1. 一句话中文介绍

一个 local-first、transcript-first 的视频分析工作流：把本地视频或公开视频 URL 转成高精度逐字稿草案、时间线、关键帧证据和结构化总结，适合把教程、录屏、技术讲解沉淀成可复核、可继续加工的知识材料。

## 2. 一句话英文介绍

A local-first, transcript-first workflow for turning local videos or public video URLs into precise transcript drafts, timeline notes, frame evidence, and structured summaries.

## 6. 中版英文介绍

I built a project called **local-video-analysis** around a simple idea:

Many video tools are good at producing a quick summary, but not so good at producing a reusable analysis package.

This workflow is local-first and transcript-first.
Instead of only generating a final summary, it tries to preserve multiple layers of reviewable output:
- transcript
- timeline
- frame evidence
- structured report
- Obsidian-friendly reading flow

It currently supports:
- local video files
- best-effort public video URL ingestion
- export into Obsidian for long-term knowledge capture

It is especially useful for:
- tutorials
- walkthroughs
- screen recordings
- technical explainers
- process review and documentation

GitHub:
https://github.com/MelloMercy/local-video-analysis

## 8. 适合什么人

适合：
- 需要复盘教程的人
- 需要保留操作证据的人
- 想把视频内容沉淀进 Obsidian / 文档库的人
- 接受“先拿高质量草案，再人工精修”的人

不太适合：
- 只想快速看个大意的人
- 不关心过程证据的人
- 不打算继续加工输出的人
